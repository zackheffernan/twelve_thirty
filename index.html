Established in 2012
the design studio Another Pony is a collective of three designers from Hamburg Germany. Working together or alone, they spend their time creating motion and stills, objects and installations for agencies, brands, science, art and culture. Eager to create new visual languages and develop their repertoire of abilities the studio is a place for research, experiments and mutual inspiration
– a playground for digital and traditional media.
